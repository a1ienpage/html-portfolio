The best movies I have ever seen

Top 3

Seven

"Seven" (1995) follows two detectives, Somerset and Mills, as they investigate a series of gruesome murders inspired by the seven deadly sins. Their pursuit leads to a chilling confrontation with the killer, culminating in a shocking and devastating finale that tests their morality and resolve.

Whiplash

"Whiplash" (2014) follows an ambitious young drummer, Andrew Neiman, who enrolls at a prestigious music conservatory and becomes determined to impress the ruthless and abusive jazz instructor, Terence Fletcher. Their intense relationship pushes Andrew to his physical and emotional limits, culminating in a powerful and electrifying final performance.

Interstellar

"Interstellar" (2014) follows a group of astronauts led by Cooper, who embark on a mission through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet as Earth faces environmental collapse. The journey explores themes of love, time, and sacrifice, culminating in a profound reunion that transcends space and time.
